S1-1/S1-2 · STEP LOAD EVENT — GRID-CONNECTED vs ISLAND MODE
SECTION 1 · GRID VS. ISLAND
Key Concept
In grid-parallel operation, the utility acts as an infinite bus. When a step load event occurs, the grid immediately supplies the extra power — the GTG output and current remain constant and undisturbed. The GTG never “sees” the load transient.
